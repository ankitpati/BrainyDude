IMPORTANT INSTRUCTIONS


  1. This is an adaptive test i.e. the difficulty of each question is based on the person's ability to give the correct answers.

  3. There are a total of 10 questions, with no negative marking.

  4. There are 9 multiple choice type questions (MCQs) with 4 alternative answers.

  5. The score of each MCQ question is dependent on the difficulty level:
    Easy: 2 marks
    Medium: 3 marks
    Hard: 5 marks

  6. The time duration of Easy, Medium and Hard MCQ questions are 2, 3 and 5 minutes respectively.

  7. The last question is a subjective type question which has to be answered within 50 words (Time Duration: 20 minutes).

  8. The subjective section will be scored on its accuracy (out of 10 marks).

  9. To save your answer and move on to the next question, click on Save & Next button.

  10. To deselect your answer, click on the Clear Response button.

  11. The question palette is displayed on the right side of the screen which shows the status of each question:


     You have answered the question.

     You are currently attempting the question.

     You have not answered the question.


  12. You cannot go back once the test is started. If you do, all your responses would be lost.

  13. You will be able to view your total score once the test is over.
