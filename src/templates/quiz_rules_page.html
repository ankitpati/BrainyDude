IMPORTANT INSTRUCTIONS


  1. This is an adaptive test i.e. the difficulty of each question is based on the person's ability to give the correct answers.

  2. Time duration of the test is 20 minutes.

  3. There are a total of 10 questions.

  4. There is no negative marking.

  5. Each question has to be answered compulsorily.

  6. There are 9 multiple choice type questions (MCQs) with 4 alternative answers.

  7. The quiz ends with a subjective type question which has to be answered within 50 words.

  8. The question palette is displayed on the right side of the screen which shows the status of each question using one of the following symbols:


     You have answered the question.

     You have not answered the question.


  9. To select your answer, select one of the 4 options.

  10. To deselect your answer, click on the Clear Response button.

  11. To save your answer, click on Save & Next button.

  12. You will be able to view your result once the test is over.
